Who I am ?

Welcome to the website ! I am Marion Mura. I created this website. I am a student in the first year of the BUT MMi at Montbéliard. This website is an exercise for the personal project of the semester.

During my free time I draw, you can see my drawings on my social networks (Instagram and Behance). I hope you are glad to meet the Mouette-Mouette drawn earlier on the page.

Understand the project

The subject is “Notes for later”. My website is “mind-interest”. I want to let notes about my interests in 2022 and I hope that it can attract other users interested in it.

The main theme of “mind-interest” is around travel and Japanese animation. A time trip with the movie because it took place during World War II.

Or a spatial trip with the articles around planes for the movie and with places for the article with the timeline of some places around Nottingham.
